How to Play

Basics

Swap two adjacent tiles to match 3 or more in a row — horizontally or vertically.

Special Tiles

4 in a row → Line blast (clears entire row or column)

5+ in a row → Color bomb (clears all tiles of one color)

T/L shape → Bomb (clears 3×3 area)

Obstacles

Ink — Has 2 layers. Damaged by adjacent matches.

Frames — Block cells. Destroyed by adjacent matches.

Locks — Tile can't be moved. Match it to unlock.

Fever Mode

Build chains and create specials to charge the fever meter. At 100%, Fever Mode activates — all points are multiplied for 5 moves!

Stars

Complete the level with moves remaining to earn stars. More moves left = more stars. Collect stars to unlock new stages!
